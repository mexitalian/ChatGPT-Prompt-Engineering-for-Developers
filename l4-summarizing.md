# Text Summarization with chatGPT

**Why:** Summarizing text is crucial in a world overwhelmed with information. It enables quick comprehension and efficient content browsing.

**How:** Utilize large language models like chatGPT to programmatically generate summaries. Customize prompts to create tailored summaries for different business departments.

**What:** This lesson illustrates how to summarize product reviews on e-commerce websites, extract pertinent details, and navigate through numerous reviews effectively.

## Cheatsheet
- Use chatGPT for text summarization
  - *Reasoning:* Efficiently handle large text volumes
  - *Example:* Summarize product reviews for quick insights
- Customize prompts for specific purposes
  - *Reasoning:* Provide relevant information for various departments
  - *Example:* Generate summaries for shipping or pricing departments
